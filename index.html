About me

I am a math postdoc at IMPAN, Warsaw, working in the group of Piotr Achinger. Previously, I was a PhD student of Hélène Esnault and Vasudevan Srinivas at FU Berlin.

I am interested in arithmetic geometry, especially étale and pro-étale fundamental groups, varieties in positive characteristic, abelian varieties and Galois representations.
