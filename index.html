About me

I am a math postdoc at GU Frankfurt in the group of Jakob Stix, while on leave at JU, Kraków.
Previously, I was a postdoc at IMPAN, Warsaw, working in the group of Piotr Achinger. Before that, I was a PhD student of Hélène Esnault and Vasudevan Srinivas at FU Berlin.

I am interested in arithmetic geometry, especially the étale and pro-étale fundamental groups, rigid geometry, condensed mathematics, varieties in positive characteristic and Galois representations.
